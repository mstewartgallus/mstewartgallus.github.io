<Body>
<Lg>
<L>A lamp hanging over head</L>
<L>The overcast sky outside</L>
<L>The felt cap across the desk</L>
<L>A man in the seat aside across from me</L>
<L>The clock 9:19 an hour behind</L>
</Lg>

<Lg>
<L>Paper scrunching, crackling</L>
<L>Fans, the <Abbr>AC</Abbr>? humming</L>
<L>Keypresses tapping</L>
</Lg>

<Lg>
<L>The textured chiclet keyboard</L>
<L>On my wrist, smooth greenwood beads</L>
<L>Soft dry socks warm my feet</L>
</Lg>

<Lg>
<L>Coffee stale on my tongue</L>
</Lg>
</Body>
